Two directory variables, TRAIN_DIR and TEST_DIR, are defined to store paths to the training and testing image directories, respectively. The TRAIN_DIR points to 'images/train' and the TEST_DIR points to 'images/test'.

TRAIN_DIR = 'images/train'
TEST_DIR = 'images/test'
