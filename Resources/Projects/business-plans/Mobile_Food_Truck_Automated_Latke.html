Customer Policies

Grand Opening

  • The grand opening of Latke On The Go will be celebrated with a special promotion offering a limited-time discount on all menu items.
  • The event will be advertised through social media, email newsletters, and local newspapers to attract a large crowd.
  • A ribbon-cutting ceremony will be held to officially mark the opening, with local dignitaries and media invited to attend.

Cancellation Policies

  • All orders must be placed at least 24 hours in advance to ensure availability of ingredients and staff.
  • Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled pickup or delivery time will incur a 50% cancellation fee.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance will incur a 100% cancellation fee.

Gift Certificate Policy

  • Gift certificates can be purchased online or in-store and are redeemable for any menu item.
  • Gift certificates are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • Gift certificates expire 6 months from the date of purchase.

Refund Policy

  • All sales are final. No refunds or exchanges will be given for any reason.
  • However, if an item is damaged or not as described, a store credit will be issued for the full amount of the purchase price.

Sales Policy

  • All prices are subject to change without notice.
  • Sales tax will be added to all orders at the time of checkout.
  • Discounts and promotions are only valid for the specified time period and cannot be combined with other offers.

Other Policies

  • Latke On The Go reserves the right to refuse service to anyone for any reason.
  • All customers must be at least 18 years old to place an order.
  • Latke On The Go is not responsible for any errors or omissions in the information provided on its website or in any marketing materials.
