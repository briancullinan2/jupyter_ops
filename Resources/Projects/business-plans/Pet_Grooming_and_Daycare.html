Operating Procedures

1. Business Operations

  • Business Hours: The Furry Fitness Center will operate from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Saturday, and 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM on Sundays.
  • Staffing: The center will be staffed by a minimum of two trained professionals at all times, including a manager or supervisor.
  • Client Intake: All clients will be required to complete a registration form and provide proof of vaccination before the first visit. This information will be kept confidential and in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.

2. Pet Grooming

  • Grooming Services: The center will offer a variety of grooming services, including baths, haircuts, nail trimming, and ear cleaning.
  • Safety Precautions: All grooming equipment will be sanitized after each use, and all staff will wear gloves and protective clothing to prevent the spread of disease.
  • Client Communication: Clients will be informed of any health issues or concerns with their pet's coat or skin before proceeding with grooming services.

3. Pet Training

  • Training Programs: The center will offer obedience training, agility training, and behavioral support services.
  • Training Sessions: All training sessions will be conducted by certified trainers, and clients will be required to sign a waiver before the first session.
  • Client Feedback: Clients will be encouraged to provide feedback on training sessions to improve services and ensure client satisfaction.

4. Eco-Friendly Practices

  • Sustainable Products: The center will use eco-friendly products for grooming and cleaning, and will implement recycling programs for waste reduction.
  • Energy Efficiency: The center will use energy-efficient lighting and equipment to minimize energy consumption.
  • Water Conservation: The center will implement water-saving measures in the grooming area to reduce water waste.

5. Health and Safety

  • Emergency Procedures: The center will have a first aid kit on site and will conduct regular fire drills to ensure staff are prepared in case of emergencies.
  • Infection Control: The center will follow strict infection control protocols to prevent the spread of disease.
  • Client Safety: The center will ensure that all areas are safe for clients and their pets, including the parking lot, entrance, and all facilities.

6. Compliance

  • Licenses and Permits: The center will maintain all necessary licenses and permits, including business, pet grooming, pet training, environmental, health and safety, zoning, building, fire safety, food service, waste management, pet care facility, animal welfare, noise control, accessibility, and tax permits.
  • Regulatory Compliance: The center will comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including those related to animal welfare, health and safety, and environmental sustainability.

7. Continuous Improvement

  • Client Feedback: The center will actively solicit feedback from clients to improve services and ensure client satisfaction.
  • Staff Training: The center will provide ongoing training for staff to ensure they are up-to-date on the latest techniques and best practices in pet care.
  • Quality Control: The center will implement quality control measures to ensure consistency in services and maintain high standards of care.

By following these operating procedures, The Furry Fitness Center aims to provide exceptional services, prioritize sustainability, and ensure compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, positioning itself as a leader in the pet care industry.

Cancellation Policies

  • Notice Period: For all services, a minimum of 24 hours' notice is required for cancellations or rescheduling. Failure to provide adequate notice will result in a cancellation fee equal to 50% of the service cost.
  • Exceptions: In the event of inclement weather or unforeseen circumstances, we reserve the right to cancel or reschedule services at our discretion.

Gift Certificate Policy

  • Purchase: Gift certificates can be purchased online or in-person at the center. They are redeemable for any service offered by The Furry Fitness Center.
  • Expiration: Gift certificates are valid for one year from the date of purchase. After this period, they can be redeemed for a gift certificate of equal value, minus any applicable fees.
  • Redemption: Gift certificates can be redeemed by presenting the physical certificate or by using the digital version sent via email.

Refund Policy

  • Services: For services that are not completed due to client cancellation or failure to provide adequate notice, a refund will be issued minus the cancellation fee.
  • Products: For products purchased at the center, returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase. Refunds will be issued in the original form of payment, minus any applicable restocking fees.

Sales Policy

  • Discounts: The Furry Fitness Center offers discounts for loyalty program members, referrals, and special promotions. Discounts cannot be combined or applied to previous purchases.
  • Price Adjustments: Prices are subject to change. If a price adjustment is made after a purchase, the customer will be notified and given the option to accept the new price or cancel the purchase.

Other Policies

  • Pet Safety: The safety and well-being of pets is our top priority. We reserve the right to refuse service to any pet that poses a risk to itself or others.
