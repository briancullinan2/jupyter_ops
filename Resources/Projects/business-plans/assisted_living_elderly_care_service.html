Management and Personnel

Silver Linings Living recognizes that our success hinges on the dedication and expertise of our team. We are committed to recruiting, training, and retaining highly qualified and compassionate individuals who share our vision of providing exceptional care and enriching experiences for our residents.

Management Team:

  • Executive Director: Responsible for overall management of the facility, including strategic planning, financial oversight, regulatory compliance, and staff supervision.
  • Assistant Director: Provides support to the Executive Director, overseeing daily operations, resident care coordination, and staff scheduling.
  • Director of Nursing: Leads the nursing team, ensuring quality care delivery, medication management, and adherence to clinical protocols.
  • Activities Director: Develops and implements engaging activities programs to promote resident well-being, social interaction, and cognitive stimulation.
  • Marketing and Admissions Director: Responsible for marketing and outreach efforts, resident recruitment, and managing the admissions process.
  • Finance Manager: Oversees financial operations, including budgeting, accounting, billing, and payroll.

Direct Care Staff:

  • Registered Nurses (RNs): Provide skilled nursing care, medication administration, and health assessments.
  • Licensed Practical Nurses (LPNs): Assist RNs with resident care, medication administration, and documentation.
  • Certified Nursing Assistants (CNAs): Provide personal care assistance, including bathing, dressing, feeding, and toileting.
  • Home Health Aides (HHAs): Assist with personal care tasks and light housekeeping.

Support Staff:

  • Dietary Staff: Prepare and serve nutritious meals, manage food safety, and accommodate dietary restrictions.
  • Housekeeping Staff: Maintain cleanliness and sanitation of the facility, including resident rooms, common areas, and laundry services.
  • Maintenance Staff: Perform repairs and maintenance tasks to ensure the safety and functionality of the facility.
  • Transportation Staff: Provide transportation services for residents to appointments, outings, and social events.
  • Receptionist/Administrative Assistant: Manage phone calls, schedule appointments, and provide administrative support.
  • Social Worker: Provide counseling, support, and resources to residents and families.
  • Activities Assistants: Assist the Activities Director in implementing activities programs.

Technology Staff:

  • IT Specialist: Manage and maintain computer systems, networks, and technology infrastructure.
